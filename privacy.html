Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Makeup Stories by Riya ("we", "us", "our") runs this website to help brides and clients across Chhattisgarh (Bhilai, Raipur, Bilaspur), Pune, Hyderabad and destination weddings throughout India enquire about and book bridal, party, and event makeup services. This policy explains what information we collect from you, how we use it, and your choices around it.

What we collect

When you fill in the enquiry form on this site, we collect what you choose to share with us:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number (so Riya can reply on WhatsApp)
  • Your wedding or function date, if you share it
  • The city or venue of your event
  • The functions you're interested in (Wedding, Reception, etc.)
  • Any notes you write about the look you want

We also automatically collect:

  • Basic technical information about your visit (browser type, device type, time of visit) — handled by our analytics tools listed below
  • How you arrived at the site (e.g. whether you clicked a Meta or Google ad) — only the source name, not your personal identity

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry personally on WhatsApp or phone
  • To prepare a package and quote based on what you've shared
  • To follow up about your booking if you've expressed interest
  • To improve our ads and website so we reach the right brides

We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not add you to mailing lists without your asking. We do not share your details with other vendors or photographers.

Where it's stored

Your enquiry details are stored privately in a Google Sheet that only Riya can access. We use Google's services (Sheets and Apps Script) to receive and store this information securely.

Tracking tools we use

Like most modern websites, we use a few standard tools to understand which ads work and which don't:

  • Meta Pixel — helps us measure how our Facebook and Instagram ads perform. It tells us if a visitor came from an ad and whether they enquired. Meta's privacy policy
  • Google Ads & Google Analytics — same purpose, for our Google ads. Google's privacy policy

These tools use cookies and similar technologies. You can opt out at any time through your browser settings or by using your phone's ad-tracking controls (Settings → Privacy → Tracking on iPhone, or Settings → Google → Ads on Android).

How long we keep it

Enquiry details are kept for as long as needed to serve you and for our business records (typically 2 years after our last contact). If you'd like your information removed sooner, just message Riya on WhatsApp and we'll delete it within 7 days.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • See what information we have about you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your information from our records

Just reach out using the contact details below.

Children

This website and our services are intended for adults arranging weddings and events. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information, please contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. For major changes, we'll add a notice on the homepage.
